Melanie

Get out of you mind and into relaxation.

Massage as a very powerful and still gentil form of communication. The body speaks during a massage, directly, personally, and without words.

My approach is rooted in deep, soft, and mindful touch. Whether you're pregnant, in a phase of hormonal change, seeking support for your cycle, or simply longing for rest and relaxation, massage offers you a space to rest.

These sessions are more than techniques, they are journeys. Journeys into stillness, into reconnection, into your own rhythm. Sometimes, it's about healing. Sometimes, it's about reorganizing what's been scattered. And sometimes, it's just about being. Your body will know the way.

You'll be welcomed into a warm, calming space with gentle music, a heated table, and nourishing oils. Together, we'll take time to understand what you need, so your experience is deeply personal, supportive, and fully yours.

I also offer scarwork. It is a way to help reintegrating scar tissue and its profound impact on the rest of the body in a very gentle though efficient way. No scar is too old. Care for them!
